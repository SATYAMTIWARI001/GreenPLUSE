# 🌍 GreenPulse – Carbon Footprint Tracker

## 📌 Chosen Vertical

**Sustainability & Climate Technology**

GreenPulse is a web-based platform designed to help individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights. The platform simplifies complex environmental data into an intuitive and interactive experience, encouraging users to adopt more sustainable habits.

---

## 💡 Approach & Logic

The solution follows a simple four-step process:

### 1. User Input
Users provide information about their daily lifestyle, including:
- Transportation habits
- Electricity usage
- Food preferences
- Water consumption
- Waste generation

### 2. Carbon Footprint Calculation
The platform estimates emissions using the standard formula:

**Carbon Footprint = Σ(Activity Value × Emission Factor)**

Each activity is converted into CO₂ equivalent (CO₂e) using publicly available average emission factors.

### 3. Analysis
The calculated emissions are categorized into:
- 🚗 Transportation
- ⚡ Electricity
- 🍽 Food
- 🗑 Waste
- 💧 Water

This helps users identify which activities contribute the most to their carbon footprint.

### 4. Personalized Insights
Based on the results, GreenPulse provides practical recommendations to reduce emissions through simple lifestyle changes, making sustainability easy to understand and implement.

## 🧠 Assumptions Made

- Emission factors are based on publicly available average values and may vary by country or region.
- User-provided information is assumed to represent their typical daily lifestyle.
- The platform is intended for awareness and educational purposes and does not replace certified carbon auditing.
- Results are estimates designed to encourage sustainable decision-making rather than provide exact environmental measurements.
